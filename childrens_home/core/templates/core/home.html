Our Story

Hope Children's Home was founded in 2005 to provide a safe and loving environment for vulnerable children in Kenya. From a small beginning, we have grown into a thriving home supporting over 50 children.

Through education, mentorship, and care, we empower children to build brighter futures and become leaders in their communities.
